What is two factor authentication?

TL;DR: You can use a number shown on a phone app along with your password to make your online accounts safer.

Two factor authentication (2FA) uses two factors to make your online accounts much safer.

When you use Facebook or Twitter, you probably just put in in your email and password, right?

This is one factor. Your email and password is something you know.

It's very easy to use sites with those things. You put them in and you're done. Sad times though. This also means it's pretty for bad people to get in to. They just need your email and password. They just need this one factor.

With 2FA, there's another thing you put in when using your favourite sites.

So, what is this second factor then?

The second factor can just be a number shown on a phone app. You put this in along with your password. Your phone is something only you have.

Bad people don't have your phone, so using it with your password gives you two factors and a very safe account.
